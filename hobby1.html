Why I love gaming

Gaming is my biggest hobby — I play most things on my Lenovo laptop. I split my time between sandbox titles like Minecraft and Roblox, and driving games. The racing games I play the most are F1 23/24/25 and Assetto Corsa, and I also enjoy Uma Musume. For the driving titles I use a wheel and pedals instead of a keyboard; that setup makes everything feel far more immersive and fun.

Assetto Corsa is what I reach for when I want realism and customisation. It's older, but the modding scene is incredible — I can grab a small mod and totally change how the game feels. I love testing different cars and tuning setups that make each session feel unique.

F1 25 is more arcade-leaning but perfect for playing with friends. My favourite mode is Co‑Op Career, where me and a mate take on objectives and build rivalries. I also spend time in Time Trial leaderboards to see how I stack up online.
